Our Leadership Team
Glen & Linda are the Commanding Officers, or head pastors, at The Salvation Army Community Church in Concord. They have long experience as pastors and administrators in The Salvation Army. They were appointed to Concord in July 2012. They are equally adept at powerful preaching, officiating at major life events such as dedication of children, marriages, funerals, and overseeing the response to the major social needs in the community.
