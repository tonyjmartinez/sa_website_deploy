Volunteering

Learn more about volunteering with the Salvation Army

Administrative Support
Assist our administrative staff with filing, photocopying, answering telephones, or serving as a receptionist.

Guest Followup
Identify and contact our first- and second-time guests.

Information Technology
We believe in using technology as a tool for accomplishing our mission. Our facilities are being updated to handle the ever-growing technology needs of our staff and teams. There are computers (both Mac & PC platforms), servers, and networking devices that require monitoring, maintenance and updating.

Worship Program
Our weekly worship bulletin not only helps our members and guests as they worship God. It is a key means of sharing information about people and events in the life of our congregation. Each Sunday morning at 9:30am this team folds and assembles our weekly worship bulletin.

Publicity
Write and distribute news releases to newspapers, television, radio, and other media.

If you are interested in helping with Administrative Support or need more information, please email our Office Manager.
Cheer Team
Our Cheer Team prays for, sends greeting cards to, visits, and prepares and gives personalized gift baskets to people in need of encouragement or support.

Community Care Ministry
Our Congregational Care Team prays for, phones, and visits members who are homebound, hospitalized, or otherwise in need of loving support and encouragement.

Prayer Team
In addition to praying for our congregation, community, and world, the Prayer Team exists to motivate and involve the entire congregation in prayer through prayer walks, prayer vigils, concerts of prayer, and other events.

Share the Care
Our Share the Care team is made up of members, attenders, and friends who are interested in cooking meals, offering rides, doing light housework, and helping people in need in simple, practical ways.


If you are interested in helping with Congregational Care or need more information, please email our Church Office.
Classroom Teacher
Prepare and present the lesson from the curriculum provided, with loving, personal attention to students.

Classroom Assistant
Help the teacher with presenting the lesson, caring for students, and classroom management.

Vacation Bible School
Oversee crafts, lead music, serve on the drama team, teach a class, prepare and serve snacks, or help oversee the program by serving on the VBS Planning Team.

Worship Leaders
Lead children in their worship as vocalists or instrumentalists.

Drama Team
Write, direct, or act in skits that reinforce the important biblical themes and practical virtues the children are learning about in their classes.

For the safety of our children, all Salvation Army volunteers must be members or adherents of Concord Community Church for at least six months and must go through a screening process that includes a written application with personal references, a criminal background check, and training and certification from The Salvation Army.

If you are interested in helping with Children's Ministry or need more information, please contact our Children's Ministry department.
Buildings and Grounds Team
The appearance of our campus and facilities communicates how much we care about God and others. Use your skills to help us maintain the interior and exterior of our campus and facilities in areas such as painting, drywall, electrical, plumbing, roofing, general carpentry and repairs.

Lawn Mowing Team
Enjoy God's creation from behind a lawn mower as you beautify our campus lawn.

Campus Work Days
Members and friends gather on Saturday mornings from time to time to tackle specific maintenance, upkeep, and improvement projects around the campus.

If you are interested in helping with the Buildings and Grounds Ministry Team or need more information, please email our Church Office.
Greeters
Greet our guests, members, and regular attenders and help them feel welcome as they arrive on campus for worship services.

Ushers
Be available for guests and members during our worship services to help them find a seat, take the offering, and address any issues that might arise.

Welcome Center
Assist guests and members before, during and after our worship services and/or special events with information, materials, and directions.

Coffee/Refreshments
It’s all about hospitality. Help set-up and serve refreshments during our weekend services and at special events.

Parking Ministry
Assist our members and guests as they arrive, help them find a convenient parking spot, and assist them as they leave the campus.

Guest Followup
Identify and contact our first- and second-time guests.

If you are interested in helping with First Impressions or need more information, please email our Church Office.
Growth Group Host/Leader
Small Group hosts are asked to do four simple things:
• Have a heart for others
• Open their homes
• Serve coffee and/or simple refreshments
• Turn on a DVD or VCR

Growth Group Ministry Team
Help oversee our Growth Group ministry program as we encourage participation by our members and attenders in growth groups, catalog our group locations and meeting times, resource our groups with curricula, and offer ongoing encouragement and training to our group hosts.

If you are interested in helping with Small Groups or need more information, please email our Growth Groups Team.
Audio Technician
Assist in operating the sound systems throughout our facilities during worship, concerts, special events, weddings, etc.

Band
Participate in our Bristish-style brass band, accompany congregational praise.

Media Technician
Prepare and run presentation software during worship services and special events..

Sermon Outreach
Record, produce, and duplicate CDs of our weekend messages.

Songsters
Sing in our adult choir.

Video Camera Operator
Run the camera during our weekend worship services, some experience required.

Worship Design and Decoration
Design and decorate our worship center for special services, message emphases, and seasonal celebrations.

Worship Team
Instrumentalists and vocalists who understand that their primary responsibility is not performance but to help lead the congregation into the presence of God.

Lighting Technician
Operate the lighting system during worship, concerts, special events, weddings, etc.

If you are interested in helping with Worship Arts or need more information, please email our Worship Arts Director.
