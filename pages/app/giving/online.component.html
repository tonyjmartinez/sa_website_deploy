How to Donate Online

 In order to protect the integrity of the organization and the privacy of our donors, The Salvation Army collects all on-line donations through a single web-based portal. Clicking one of the
buttons on any page will transfer you from this site to the specialized collection site.

 Since this collection site handles donations world-wide, unless you specify clearly that you wish your donation to be used locally, it may be used for the general, on-going work of The Army in other regions.
