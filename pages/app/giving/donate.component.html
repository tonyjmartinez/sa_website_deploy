In-Kind Donations

 If you would like to donate clothing, furniture and household items, please select the option below for connection to our thrift stores, which benefit The Salvation Army’s adult rehabilitation program.

To Schedule a Material Goods Pick Up or Car Donation:
  • Call 1-800-SA-TRUCK (1-800-728-7825)
  • Visit satruck.org.

Contact us to discuss a pick up of your items or to find out the nearest drop off location.

Planned Giving

 Bequests and other testamentary gifts have been a significant source of support for Salvation Army programs and services for over 140 years.
 The world’s problems aren’t easily solved, but gifts help build solutions. Donors can make a difference in the lives that follow by including The Salvation Army in their estate plan. The lives that are changed through The Salvation Army are part of a legacy. Please visit our website at www.salegacygifts.org for more details.
