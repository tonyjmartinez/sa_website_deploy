Top Ten Reasons to Give to The Salvation Army

  1. Its Christ-centered, Bible based approach is the best way to bring about lasting change.
  2. Its holistic ministry focuses on body, mind and spirit.
  3. The Army will maximize your contributions to help the most people.
  4. You can be assured that you are making a meaningful difference in your community.
  5. The organization’s services and processes are overseen by a community volunteer board to ensure accountability.
  6. It has a track record of staying true to its mission for over a century.
  7. Salvation Army workers are passionate about improving the lives of men, women, boys, and girls.
  8. Its vast portfolio of compassionate services offers help for children, families and senior citizens.
  9. It has an unparalleled record of successful results.
  10. Its strategic plan ensures expansion of services to keep pace with population growth.
